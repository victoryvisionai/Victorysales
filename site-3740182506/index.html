A Timeless Symbol of the River

The Queen isn't just a boat — she's a beloved landmark and a source of pride for generations of Baton Rouge locals. From festive voyages to twilight cruises, she’s carried the rich cadence of Southern life on the Mississippi for decades.

Red paddle wheels turning under cotton-candy sunsets, the echo of a passing train downriver, laughter spilling from the deck — all memories that make her legacy live on in every heart she's touched.
