Welcome Aboard The Queen

A Baton Rouge legend returns — where heritage meets hospitality. Join us as Bally’s breathes new life into this icon of the Mississippi.

A Timeless Symbol of the River

The Queen isn't just a boat — she's a memory, a landmark, and a source of pride for generations of Baton Rouge locals. Since her first glides down the Mississippi, she’s carried not just guests, but stories, culture, music, and hope.

Red paddle wheels turning under Southern skies, the call of the riverbank train, and laughter spilling from the deck — these are the rhythms of her legacy.

A New Chapter with Bally's

Bally’s is honored to steward the next era of The Queen, blending her storied past with new energy, entertainment, and community. Plans for her revitalization include immersive experiences, riverside dining, live music, and more — all rooted in the vibrant soul of Baton Rouge.

This isn’t just a destination — it’s a comeback for a riverboat legend, driven by passion and powered by progress.
