The 7-Day Follow-Up Cadence That Doesn't Annoy Prospects

Victory Vision's proven sequence for property management outreach. Calibrated for relationship-sensitive real estate conversations.

Day 1 — First Touch

Personalized Email: The Relevant Insight

AI pulls a specific, relevant market insight about the prospect's property or portfolio. The email leads with value, not an ask. Response rate benchmark: 8–12%.

Day 2 — Multi-Channel

LinkedIn Connection Request

A brief, personal note that references the email. Multi-channel approach significantly increases visibility and response rates for professional audiences.

Day 3 — Value Follow-Up

Email: The Data Point They Can Use

No ask. Just a specific piece of market data relevant to their situation—vacancy rates, rent trends, or management fee benchmarks in their submarket. Positions you as the knowledgeable local expert.

Day 5 — The Ask

Email: One Clear CTA

"15 minutes to see if what we do makes sense for your situation." A single Calendly link. No friction. This is where 40–60% of eventual meetings are booked.

Day 7 — Final Touch

Email: Leave the Door Open

Brief, low-pressure close. "Not the right time—no worries. Happy to reconnect in Q[X]." Prospect is automatically moved to a 90-day slow-drip nurture sequence. Never disappears from your pipeline.
