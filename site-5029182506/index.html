The Queen Returns

A Riverboat Like No Other

Since her christening, The Queen has graced the Baton Rouge riverfront as a timeless icon. From spinning roulette wheels to live jazz echoing into the night, she’s been a heart of entertainment under the southern stars.

With her signature blue roof and paddlewheel charm, The Queen was more than a casino—it was where memories were made, stories were shared, and history came alive on the Big Muddy.

Echoes of the Past

Locals remember the riverwalk fireworks, steamboat dinners, and the rhythmic hum of the passing train. Generations laughed, danced, and placed their bets in an atmosphere where southern hospitality met timeless thrill.

“There was magic in stepping aboard,” shared longtime Baton Rougean Mary Lou Franklin. “You felt connected to something grand – and homegrown.”

Something New on the Horizon

The spirit of The Queen will live on – bigger, bolder, and more vibrant than ever. We're excited to announce our next chapter: a new development in Baton Rouge that reimagines casino luxury while honoring riverboat roots.

From immersive gaming halls and curated culinary experiences, to performance venues anchored in Gulf Coast style—every detail has been crafted to reflect the soul of the Mississippi while charting a course toward tomorrow.
