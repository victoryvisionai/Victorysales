The Bottom Line

HubSpot is a powerful platform built for tens of thousands of B2B companies across every industry. That breadth is also its weakness for property management: it doesn't know what a cap rate is, doesn't identify property owners, and requires months of configuration to get close to what Victory Vision ships on day one.

If you're already paying for HubSpot and using it primarily as a contact database with manual sequences, you're likely spending 10x more for 20% of what Victory Vision delivers for PM-specific prospecting.

When HubSpot makes more sense: You run a large multi-vertical organization with enterprise CRM needs, complex revenue operations requirements, and a dedicated RevOps team to configure and maintain it.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use Victory Vision alongside HubSpot?

Yes. Victory Vision can feed qualified, meeting-ready prospects into HubSpot for pipeline management and deal tracking. Some firms use Victory Vision for top-of-funnel prospecting and HubSpot as their deal CRM. However, most PM firms find that Victory Vision's built-in attribution and pipeline tracking makes the HubSpot overlap redundant.

How much does HubSpot actually cost for sales automation?

HubSpot's Sales Hub Starter starts at $45/mo per seat but lacks sequences and many automation features. Sales Hub Professional (the tier needed for full sequence automation and attribution) starts at $450/mo for 5 users. Enterprise is $1,200+/mo. Compare that to Victory Vision's $99–$799/mo all-in pricing with PM-specific features.

Can Victory Vision import my existing HubSpot contacts?

Yes. Victory Vision can import your existing contact database from HubSpot (or any CSV export) so you're not starting from scratch. Your existing pipeline data transfers without a complex migration.

What about HubSpot's free CRM?

HubSpot's free tier is genuinely useful as a contact database. But it doesn't include sequences, AI, or pipeline automation—the features you'd need to replace manual outreach. By the time you add those capabilities, you're in the $800–$1,500+/mo range where Victory Vision offers far more PM-specific value per dollar.
