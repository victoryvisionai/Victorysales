The Sacred Mission

As members of the Long Gray Line, it is our sacred duty to support the families of our fallen classmates. These scholarships will provide crucial educational opportunities for their children, honoring their memory and ensuring their sacrifice continues to serve our nation through the next generation.

We who survived carry the responsibility to ensure that the children of those who gave their lives in service to our country receive the same opportunities for excellence that West Point instilled in us all.

Every Contribution Honors Their Service

The Lasting Impact

These scholarships will directly support the educational journeys and career aspirations of our fallen classmates' children, ensuring they have access to the same caliber of education and opportunities that shaped us at West Point.

Recipients will join a network of support that extends far beyond financial assistance, connecting them to mentorship, internships, and career guidance from the Class of 2000 and the broader West Point community.

How to Serve

Corporate Leadership: Partner with us through major gift commitments and employee engagement programs that honor military service.

Individual Commitment: Join your classmates in monthly giving, memorial donations, or one-time contributions that fit your financial capacity.

Network Activation: Leverage your professional and personal networks to expand our reach and impact.

Accountability & Transparency

In keeping with our West Point values, we commit to complete transparency in all fundraising and distribution activities. Regular progress reports will detail funding milestones, scholarship awards, and recipient outcomes.

A board of classmates will oversee all operations, ensuring every dollar contributed directly serves our mission of honoring our fallen and supporting their families.
