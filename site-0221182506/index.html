A Rich History on the River

For over three decades, The Queen has been Baton Rouge's premier casino destination, floating majestically on the Mississippi River. Our storied history began in 1987 when Louisiana first embraced riverboat gaming, bringing excitement and entertainment to the heart of Louisiana's capital city.

Named after the grand riverboats that once plied these waters during the golden age of Mississippi River commerce, The Queen has maintained its commitment to Southern hospitality while evolving with the times. From our humble beginnings as a modest riverboat to our current status as Louisiana's most beloved gaming destination, we've always honored our heritage.

Throughout the years, The Queen has weathered storms both literal and figurative, always emerging stronger and more committed to serving our community. We've been witness to Baton Rouge's growth and have proudly contributed to the economic vitality of our great city.

Today, as we embark on a new chapter with our state-of-the-art facility, we carry forward the traditions that have made The Queen a cherished institution. Our commitment to excellence, community, and the timeless spirit of Southern hospitality remains unwavering as we write the next chapter of our river legacy.
