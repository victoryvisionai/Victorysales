A Storied Legacy on the River

Since the early days of steamboat glitz and riverfront revelry, The Queen has stood as a symbol of Baton Rouge's vibrant spirit. Anchored in tradition and community, this riverboat casino has welcomed generations of locals, travelers, and players, offering not just games of chance but memories for a lifetime.

Born from the romance of paddlewheels and the grandeur of Mississippi nights, The Queen carved its place in Louisiana’s cultural tapestry. From historic jazz nights echoing against her railings, to the first jackpots won under her chandeliers, her story has always been one of legacy and laughter.

The Next Chapter: Reimagined Elegance

As we steer into the future, The Queen rises anew — redefined with refined entertainment, cutting-edge gaming experiences, and unparalleled Southern hospitality. Opening soon in Baton Rouge, the new Queen will be a destination that honors her heritage while providing amenities that satisfy today’s traveler and thrill seeker.

Step aboard a fresh experience: From rooftop lounges overlooking the river to curated dining rooted in Louisiana flavor, from modern architecture that nods to the past to immersive entertainment that celebrates local talent — the new Queen is more than revival. She’s a renaissance.
